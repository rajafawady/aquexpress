Frequently Asked Questions

How do I place an order for water delivery?

To place an order, simply download the app or use our web version, log in to your AquExpress account, select your preferred delivery time and quantity, and choose a payment method. It's quick and easy!

Can I track my order in real-time?

Yes, you can! AquExpress offers real-time order tracking. You'll receive updates at every stage of the delivery process, from order confirmation to delivery completion.

How do I reset my password?

Click “Forgot password” from the login page or “Change password” from your profile page. A reset link will be emailed to you.

What is the advantage of automatic ordering?

Automatic ordering lets you set up recurring deliveries based on your schedule, whether it's weekly, bi-weekly, or monthly. This ensures a hassle-free and uninterrupted water supply without the need for manual ordering.

How do I choose a water supplier?

AquaExpress provides a variety of water suppliers. From mobile app you can choose based on factors such as reliability, pricing, and delivery speed. Our extensive supplier network empowers you to make informed choices.

What if I have questions or issues with my order?

We're here to help! Our dedicated support team is ready to assist you. You can reach out to us for any questions, concerns, or assistance you may need during your AquaExpress experience.
